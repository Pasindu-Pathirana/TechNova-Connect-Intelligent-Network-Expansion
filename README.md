# TechNova-Connect-Intelligent-Network-Expansion

Scenario: 

TechNova Ltd. is a leading software and consulting company expanding its network infrastructure. The company is divided into several departments, each with its own subnetwork and connectivity requirements.

Here are the number of computers in each subnetwork:

a. Finance Department - 60 computers
b. Research Department - 40 computers
c. Administration Department - 15 computers
d. Sales Department - 25 computers
e. Logistics Department - 12 computers

The network is structured as follows:

•	Router X is connected to Router Y and Router Z
•	The Finance Department LAN is connected to Router X.
•	The Research Department LAN is connected to Router Y.
•	The Administration Department LAN is connected to Router Z.
•	The Sales Department LAN is connected to Router Z as well.
•	The Logistics Department LAN is connected to Router W, which is also connected to Router Y.
•	The company’s Intranet Application Server is connected to the Finance Department LAN.

You have been provided with the IP Address Block of 10.10.0.0/24 for LAN connections and 192.168.50.0/24 for inter-router connections.
