# TechNova-Connect-Intelligent-Network-Expansion

Scenario: 

TechNova Ltd. is a leading software and consulting company expanding its network infrastructure. The company is divided into several departments, each with its own subnetwork and connectivity requirements.<br>

Here are the number of computers in each subnetwork:<br>


a. Finance Department - 60 computers<br>

b. Research Department - 40 computers<br>

c. Administration Department - 15 computers<br>

d. Sales Department - 25 computers<br>

e. Logistics Department - 12 computers<br>


The network is structured as follows:<br>


•	Router X is connected to Router Y and Router Z<br>

•	The Finance Department LAN is connected to Router X.<br>

•	The Research Department LAN is connected to Router Y.<br>

•	The Administration Department LAN is connected to Router Z.<br>

•	The Sales Department LAN is connected to Router Z as well.<br>

•	The Logistics Department LAN is connected to Router W, which is also connected to Router Y.<br>

•	The company’s Intranet Application Server is connected to the Finance Department LAN.<br>


You have been provided with the IP Address Block of 10.10.0.0/24 for LAN connections and 192.168.50.0/24 for inter-router connections.<br>
